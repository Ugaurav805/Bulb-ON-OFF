What can a bulb do?

  • A light bulb produces light from electricity.
  • The bulb is designed to charge even during normal operation when it is turned on.
  • A bulb can light up the room.
  • A bulb can be used as a decoration.
  • A bulb can be used as a signal.
  • A bulb can be used as a light source.
  • A bulb can be used as a light source for a camera.
